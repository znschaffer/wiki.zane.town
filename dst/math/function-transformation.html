Function Transformation

IHOP
Inside, Horizontal, Opposite

Af(B(x+C))+DAf(B(x + C)) + DAf(B(x+C))+D

AAA: Vertical Stretch/Compression

BBB: Horizontal Stretch/Compression

CCC: Horizontal Shift

DDD: Vertical Shift

Shifts

Seen through addition or subtraction either “inside” (horizontal) or “outside” (vertical) the function.

f(x)=x2+2f(x) = x^2 + 2f(x)=x2+2 has a vertical shift up 2 units

f(x)=(x−4)2f(x) = (x - 4)^2f(x)=(x−4)2 has a horizontal shift right 4 units

Stretches and Compressions

Seen through multiplication either “inside” (horizontal) or “outside” (vertical) the function.

f(x)=2(x)2f(x) = 2(x)^2f(x)=2(x)2 is vertically stretched by 2.

f(x)=(2x)2f(x) = (2x)^2f(x)=(2x)2 is horizontally compressed by 2.

Combining Transformations

When dealing with multiple transformations, factor the equation out to Af(B(x+C))+DAf(B(x+C))+DAf(B(x+C))+D to help with ordering.
